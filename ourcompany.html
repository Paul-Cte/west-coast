    • Who are We

    West Coast Bees was founded in 2015 by Nathanael, Maceo, and Thomas, who share a deep passion for bees and sustainability. They created the company with a mission to promote environmental conservation and community education. Starting with just a few beehives, their commitment to making a positive impact on the environment has driven their journey. Through their dedication, West Coast Bees has grown into a business that offers high-quality, sustainable bee products and educates the community about the importance of bees. Their efforts have established West Coast Bees as a leader in sustainability and environmental stewardship. 🌿🐝

    What we do

    Starting with a small apiary, we produce raw, unfiltered honey that captures the essence of the West Coast's diverse flora. Our product line has expanded to include beeswax candles, propolis tinctures, royal jelly supplements, and bee-friendly gardening kits. Each product is crafted with care, ensuring benefits for both consumers and the environment. One of our proudest initiatives is the "Adopt a Hive" program, allowing customers to sponsor a beehive and receive regular updates and a share of the honey produced. This program reflects our commitment to transparency and community engagement. 🌿🐝

    Our passion

    At West Coast Bees, our passion for bees and their vital role in our ecosystem drives everything we do. We are dedicated to sustainable practices and innovation, striving to create a world where bees and humans thrive together in harmony. Despite facing challenges such as unpredictable weather and scaling a sustainable business, our unwavering dedication has seen us through. Today, West Coast Bees stands as a beacon of sustainability in the world of bee products, with a thriving online store, loyal customer base, and numerous environmental accolades. 🌿🐝
